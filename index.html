愿景Our Vision
HOLLA Group旗下产品用户遍及全球100多个国家城市，为过千万的用户构建真实的、沉浸式的交友生态。赋予用户及时交友、表达自我和享受当下的乐趣。HOLLA Group aims to connect users around the world in more authentic and engaging ways by empowering them to instantly meet new people, express themselves, enjoy the moment, and have fun together.
世界地图World Map
HOLLA Group旨在赋能用户新的、打破物理世界限制的交友方式。旗下产品连接了全球87个国家/地区，为超过2000万用户提供真实、健康、有趣的陌生人交友体验。We envision a world where discovering new people are no longer bounded by the limitations of the physical world. Through our products, we serve more than 20M users by connecting users in authentic and engaging ways.
HOLLA Group（达摩英时网络科技有限公司）成立于2016年下半年，是一家主打海外市场的移动互联网公司。公司旨在通过旗下的社交产品矩阵，为下一代移动互联网用户提供真实、健康、有趣的陌生人交友体验。HOLLA Group was founded in 2016. Our products are available in more than 100 countries and have been translated into 20 different languages. At HOLLA Group, we pride ourselves in our diverse, innovative, and tenacious company culture. That’s the secret behind HOLLA Group’s product innovation and success.
